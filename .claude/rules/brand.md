# Brand Rules

Source of truth: Drive Docs/Sonar Brand Guidelines.pdf

## Brand essence
Sonar is a signal-based GTM intelligence company. We cut through noise, detect movement, and help sales teams act when timing matters.

Personality: Sharp, calm, curious, occasionally witty. Premium without being stiff.
Promise: Better timing, relevance, prioritization, and pipeline focus.
Enemy: Noise, fake personalization, tool sprawl, empty AI hype, volume worship.

## Voice
We sound like a smart operator who sees the pattern, tells the truth, and makes the next move obvious.

Qualities: Confident. Concise. Commercial. Curious. Lightly irreverent.

We do NOT sound like: AI-bro hustle. SDR outsourcing. Crypto cyberpunk. Enterprise jargon soup.

## Language rules
Say this:
- Signal-based GTM intelligence
- Buying windows
- Commercial relevance
- Explainable prioritization
- Human-guided agents
- Move when timing matters

Never say:
- AI SDRs
- Guaranteed meetings
- Fully autonomous sales
- Replace your SDR team
- Blast more leads
- Unleash domination
- Crush / Dominate / 10x guaranteed / Army of agents

## Approved copy lines
- Find the signal. Move when it matters.
- Stop working the whole ocean.
- More activity is not more intelligence.
- The market is already talking. Sonar tells you what matters.
- Your CRM remembers the past. Sonar watches what is moving now.
- Pipeline starts before the reply.

## Typography
- Sora Bold / ExtraBold — headlines, titles, hero statements, category language
- Inter Regular / Medium — body copy, labels, bullets, captions, docs

## Visual language
Use: waves, echoes, pulses, concentric rings, data currents, negative space, one active signal point
Avoid: crosshairs, targeting reticles, weapons, submarines, military maps, robot faces, crypto/hacker clutter

Rule: Dark slides create atmosphere. Light slides explain. Do not make every slide cinematic.

## Differentiators
- Intelligence before outreach — we ask what changed, what it means, and whether action is justified
- Buying windows not leads — a buying window combines fit, movement, timing, context, and value
- Explainable action — always answer: why this account, why this person, why now, what happens next

## Brand mantra
Less noise. More signal. Better timing.
That is the bar for every deck, page, post, proposal, and product surface.
