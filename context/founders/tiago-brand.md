## PART 1 — BRAND STRATEGY

### Who he is for
Primary audience: VPs Sales, CROs, Heads of Revenue at B2B SaaS and tech-enabled services (Series A/B, 10–250 employees). They have the tools — HubSpot, Apollo, Sales Navigator — but pipeline is still inconsistent and the team is burning out on activity without results.

Secondary audience: Founders doing founder-led sales who need to build their first scalable outbound motion before hiring a full team.

Their #1 frustration: "We're paying for all the tools. Why is pipeline still unpredictable?"

### What he stands for
The VP who stopped grinding pipeline manually and built the machine instead. Not a coach who teaches theory — a practitioner who did the hard work and then systematized it. He speaks to revenue leaders as a peer, not an authority looking down.

### The enemy
Volume worship. Fake personalization ("Saw your post!"). Tool sprawl without a coherent signal layer. Empty AI hype that overpromises and underdelivers. The SDR hamster wheel.

### The promise
Better timing beats more volume. A signal-based system that tells you which accounts deserve attention right now — and why — is worth more than 10x the email volume.
