# Sonar GTM — Company Context

## What we are
Sonar is an AI systems company. The first packaged system is revenue intelligence — a signal-to-action GTM engine. Revenue is the starting point, not the limit.

We are not recreating technology. We are removing the friction between market signals and commercial action. The tools already exist. Teams can't stitch them together into something that tells a coherent story.

## The problem we solve
Modern GTM has a signal problem, not a lead problem:
- Too much noise, not enough commercial signal
- Tools that don't talk to each other
- Outreach that ignores timing and context
- No shared logic between marketing and sales
- Teams that can't explain why a prospect deserves attention right now

## What Sonar is — system architecture
Three modes, one closed loop:

LISTEN — always-on capture from web, CRM, social, hiring, product, content, visitors, account movement
INTELLIGENCE CORE — resolve identity, infer context, score relevance, select plays, route action, record outcomes
PULSE — directed activation around a vertical, account set, event, paid campaign, or market hypothesis

The delivery loop: Discover → Design → Build → Operate → Improve

## ICP (first wedge)
Primary target: Founder-led B2B SaaS and tech-enabled services
- Headcount: 20–150 employees
- ACV: $15K+
- Stack: existing CRM + outbound tools (HubSpot, Apollo, Sales Navigator)
- Pain: inconsistent pipeline despite having the right tools
- Decision maker: founder, CRO, VP Sales, or Head of Revenue

Trigger conditions that make them ready to buy:
- New revenue leader hired
- SDR/AE/RevOps hiring happening now
- Funding event or market expansion
- Product launch or new vertical
- Dormant CRM activity that's not being worked

NOT a fit: no defined ICP, ACV under $5K, no sales owner, no CRM, believes volume fixes everything

## Offer architecture

### Deploy Sonar (core offer — revenue teams)
A managed system that listens for market movement and runs directed activation around ICP, account signals, CRM history, and timing.
Includes: signal architecture + data sources + context/scoring/"why now" logic + research agents + message inputs + CRM tasks/alerts/sequences/reporting + managed monitoring and improvement

### Build with Sonar (expansion offer — trusted clients)
Custom AI systems for high-value workflows beyond GTM: proposals, onboarding, research, reporting, customer success, operational coordination.
Includes: workflow diagnosis + custom agents + integrations + human approvals + governance + ongoing operating support + reusable system components
