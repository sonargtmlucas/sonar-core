# Sonar Brand — Quick Reference

Full brand rules: .claude/rules/brand.md
Source deck: Drive Docs/Sonar Brand Guidelines.pdf

## One-line positioning
Signal-based GTM intelligence that helps B2B sales teams act when timing matters.

## Voice in one sentence
A smart operator who sees the pattern, tells the truth, and makes the next move obvious.

## Primary colors
- Background: #071A2B (Abyss Navy)
- Primary accent: #1CB7FF (Signal Blue)
- Text: #F7FAFC (Polar White)
- Muted text: #B8C4CE (Sonar Silver)
- Borders: #334452 (Graphite)
- Alert: #FF6B57 (Pulse Coral)

## Typography
- Headlines: Sora Bold / ExtraBold
- Body: Inter Regular / Medium

## Default system: Abyss Minimal (dark, premium, cinematic)
Use for: sales decks, founder posts, category narrative
Daylight Sonar (light) for: docs, reports, onboarding
Neon Current (high energy) for: launches and social moments only

## Approved lines (use these verbatim)
- Find the signal. Move when it matters.
- Pipeline starts before the reply.
- Stop working the whole ocean.
- Your CRM remembers the past. Sonar watches what is moving now.
- The market is already talking. Sonar tells you what matters.
- More activity is not more intelligence.

## Hard no
No "crush" / "dominate" / "10x guaranteed" / "army of agents"
No crosshairs, submarines, robot faces, crypto aesthetics
No guaranteed meetings, no "replace your SDR team"
